What is Acidtag?
Protection-first technology for an over-loaded information world.

Verification layer, not just a label

Acidtag acts as a verification checkpoint between a claim and your belief — using tags, codes, and digital records to confirm what’s real.

Designed for everyday use

From products on shelves to documents in your inbox, Acidtag is built so regular people can check truth without needing tech expertise.

Built for a future of deepfakes

As AI-generated content explodes, Acidtag’s goal is simple: create a trusted way to prove authenticity before harm can spread.

How Acidtag works
From scan to signal — in seconds.
1 • Tag A product, document, or digital asset receives an Acidtag — physical or digital — tied to a secured record.
2 • Scan The user scans the tag (phone, device, or integrated system) and requests verification.
3 • Verify Acidtag checks signatures, history, and claims against trusted records and fraud patterns.
4 • Signal The user receives a clear signal: aligned, suspicious, or unverified — before they commit.
